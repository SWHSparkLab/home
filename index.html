Who Are We?

SparkNC is driven by the collaborative efforts of the SparkNC Team, SparkLab Leaders, our Board of Directors, our Business Advisory Council, and a dedicated network of engaged districts, industry and education partners. Together, these groups guide and shape SparkNC's mission-driven work.
